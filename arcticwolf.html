Introduction

This is the post explaining my third work term at Arctic Wolf Networks in Waterloo. This was my third co-op and my first real introduction to being a developer in a professional environment. This post should help you see what I learned and how I grew as a developer over the 4 months I was there.

Employer Info

Arctic Wolf Networks is Cyber Security company that offers SOC (Security Operations Center) as a Service. They collect logs, packets, and other data, analyze this data and then provide it to a Security Engineer to take a look at. They are currently a company of ~250 people but are quickly growing. All of R&D is based out of Waterloo, but they also have employees in California, Utah, and in Minnesota.
